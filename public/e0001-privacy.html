Summary

E0001 Cookie Bridge is a companion extension for Data Box (P0020) Cookie Bridge. It reads and writes browser cookies only when you explicitly choose Sync or Load on a configured route. We do not sell data, run ads, or track browsing for marketing.

What we do not do

  • No analytics or advertising SDKs
  • No background cookie harvesting — sync is manual per route
  • No sharing cookie data with third parties except your Data Box Supabase backend
  • No sale of personal data

Permissions

  • cookies — read/write on explicit Sync or Load for configured domains
  • storage — local auth, bindings, preferences
  • tabs — open target site after Load (optional)
  • <all_urls> — required by chrome.cookies API for user-configured domains

Data retention & deletion

Vault data follows your Data Box note lifecycle. Uninstalling the extension removes local storage for that Chrome profile.

Security

Full cookie jars are encrypted client-side (AES-GCM) before upload. Transport uses HTTPS.
